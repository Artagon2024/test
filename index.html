Team of Artagon

AKILA MUNIYARAJ

My artwork focuses on themes of creation, change, and beauty, exploring the transformative power of overcoming obstacles. By turning hardships into something beautiful, I highlight resilience and growth. It inspires viewers to find hope and creativity in the face of adversity.

ANANTHU DEVADOSS

My artwork seamlessly blends abstraction and landscape, inviting viewers to dive deeper into the exploration of perception, meaning, and imagination. Specializing in contemporary and oil painting, I create visually captivating yet thought-provoking scenes that inspire reflection and spark meaningful conversations with the world around us.

CHINMAYA DEVY

As a textile designer from Pondicherry, my work blends nature's beauty with the diverse experiences of women. Through vibrant colors and textures, I capture everyday life and the human experience. I see art as a medium to unite people and celebrate life in all its forms.

DEEPAK RAJARAMAN

Deepak’s artistic journey, inspired by his mother, features a unique figurative style that blends abstraction and figuration. His vibrant work primarily celebrates women, highlighting beauty and empowerment. Through a dynamic and intuitive process, he creates multiple pieces simultaneously, reflecting his evolving artistic vision.

DINESH ALAGURAJ

Dinesh, also known as Deen, developed a passion for art early on in Chennai, sketching cars and portraits. He studied multimedia, painting, and game design, gaining expertise in 3D modeling and animation. Now, he seeks to explore new creative paths while returning to his artistic roots.

GOKULA VARNA SANTHANAM

My artistic journey began young, with art remaining a constant amid various hobbies. Inspired by cyberpunk media and Hidetaka Miyazaki’s themes, I pursued a Fine Arts diploma to deepen my skills. Despite personal challenges, I am drawn to digital realms like game design, where art and technology merge to tell powerful stories.

JAISRIRAM RAJKUMAR

As a Game Designer, I create educational tech games that turn learning into interactive adventures for children. My art spans various mediums, from stone to 3D modeling, exploring complex emotions in both physical and digital forms. I also specialize in monochrome portrait photography, capturing identity and emotion through light and shadow.

JEEVITHA SUBRAMANI

Jeevitha, a Chennai-based artist, has viewed art as a lifelong companion and a path to self-discovery. Though her journey through a bachelor's in painting was challenging, it deepened her connection to painting as a means of personal growth. Each artwork reflects her evolving sense of self, expressing both her journey and transformation.

JOYCE SHIJU

The power of color has always fascinated me, leading to a love for watercolor's fluidity and murals' impact. My journey grew from doodles to immersive experiences blending realism and abstraction, inspired by nature and contemporary art. Each piece seeks to evoke emotion and transform spaces, making art a powerful form of expression.

KOPPAMMAL KOUSALYA

As a textile designer focused on Kalamkari and natural dyeing, I blend tradition with modernity in my work. My vibrant designs tell stories of mythology and culture, using eco-friendly materials and sustainable practices. Recognized for their unique fusion, my creations express cultural identity and personal narratives.

KARTHIKEYAN KAMALAKANNAN

As a passionate artist, I express my voice through mural painting and detailed pencil work. At "MURAL MAGIC," I transform spaces with vibrant murals and intricate drawings. Each project collaborates with clients, reflecting their vision and the belief that art inspires and connects.

KAVIPRIYA THANGARAJ

My art reflects my soul, celebrating nature's beauty through painting, textiles, and sculpture. Inspired by flowers as symbols of growth and resilience, I blend traditional and contemporary methods. I explore their beauty and emotional cycles, celebrating their role in nature and human experience.

LALITHALAKSHMI PARAMASIVAM

As a fine artist, my curiosity drives me to translate experiences into vibrant works of art. Inspired by diverse encounters, I capture the energy of city streets and the beauty of nature. I also mentor aspiring creatives, fostering a community that nurtures the next generation of artists.

POOJA VINOTHKUMAR

I’m Pooja V, and during my diploma at ASAS Academy of Fine Arts, I’ve gained invaluable skills that deepen my understanding of art. Art is my sanctuary, allowing me to explore my inner world, and my experience as a classical dancer ignited a desire to share the profound connection that art and dance can offer.

PRAKASH ESWARAMOORTHY

Prakash's rural upbringing and life experiences shape his artwork, especially after losing his father as a child. Finding comfort in the arts, he captures the essence of daily life. Now at "Pattam," a children's newspaper, he inspires young people while celebrating the beauty of human experiences.

SAGUNA CHANDRASEKAR

A young artist chose Visual Arts over traditional careers, turning her passion into a successful journey. Inspired by Indian art, architecture, and nature, she captures the interplay of innovation and tradition. Her work blends the past and present, offering a fresh perspective on cultural legacy and modern expression.

MOHITHA VINOTHKUMAR

I’m Mohitha, a student at the ASAS Academy of Fine Arts, pursuing a Certificate course. My art is guided by emotion, using various media to express whatever inspires me in the moment. I find joy in sharing my colorful creations, bringing vibrancy to the world around me.

SARALADEVI VINOTHKUMAR

I recently completed a diploma course at ASAS Academy of Fine Arts, where I discovered my true passion for drawing. It took time to realize that art is within reach for all of us, even in everyday activities. My message to women is simple: if I can do it, you can too.

SARAVANACHANDRU BASKARAN

Mr. Saravanachandru is a gifted watercolor painter known for capturing the tranquility of nature, rural life, and the grandeur of the Yelagiri mountains. His delicate brushstrokes and attention to detail transport viewers to serene landscapes. Through the fluidity of watercolor, he masterfully evokes the softness of foggy mornings, vivid sunsets, and the beauty of nature.

SASIKUMAR AMMASI

As a young artist from Kotagiri in the Nilgiri Hills, I incorporate rust stains into my work, blending nature with art. Rust art intrigues me for its environmentally friendly approach and the beauty found in corrosion and oxidation. This evolving medium challenges traditional boundaries, offering a fresh perspective on art.

SHWETHA MOSES

Injuries shifted my focus from sports to art, where I found a profound connection. Painting became my medium to express thoughts and emotions, turning passion into purpose. Through my art, I aim to spark conversations, challenge perspectives, and share my unique vision with the world.

SRINIVASAN MANI

An artist, Assistant Professor, and YouTuber, I have been passionate about art since high school, specializing in painting with soft pastels. With over 15 years of experience, I create portraits that capture the essence of people. Through my art school UYIROVIYAM and YouTube channel LIVE ART CHENNAI, I share my knowledge globally.

SUDARSON RAMANI

As a sportsperson, I discovered pencil carving during recovery from a badminton injury, and it became my true calling. Inspired by artists like Salavat Fidai, I believe anyone can learn this art with consistent practice. I am SUDARSON, the artist behind i_carve_pencil, and I cherish every carving I've created.

SUDHA RAJENDRAN

DR Sudha Rajendran, a Chennai-based artist, brings feminist narratives to life through vibrant acrylic paintings. Inspired by Fauvism, her impasto technique adds depth and texture, highlighting the strength of women. With a Masters in Fine Arts, she continues to push the boundaries of contemporary art.

YOKESH KRISHNAN RAVICHANDRAN

As an emerging artist and designer, I’m excited to present my first fine art photography exhibition, showcasing my unique perspective on street life, architecture, and temples. My work transforms ordinary scenes into extraordinary narratives, inviting viewers to experience the beauty and meaning behind each frame.
